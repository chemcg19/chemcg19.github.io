### 2024 Game Jam
This was my very first group project, leading a group of 6 people, we created a 2D pixel art platformer game with various features such as dialogue, item pickups, win criteria, enemy patrolling, and platform moving. The game features 2 levels. Every in-game mechanic was coded by me, with other group members mainly contributing with animations, sprites, start and end menu artwork, and background contribution such as moodboards for inspiration. Communicating well with my team and implementing ideas, changes, and importing their assets made for a smooth development process and a satisfying submission day.
