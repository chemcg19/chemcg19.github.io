## First Year
### 2D Platform Adventure Game

This is a solo 2D project that took 2-3 months to develop. The game features basic movement mechanics, animated pathfinding enemies that do damage, and a personal follower, acting as a light source for the player. Thoughtful unique mechanics and atmospheric lighting effects earned approval from examiners.
